M42 - "Orion Nebula" - NGC 1976

Location

R.A. = 05h 35m 17.3s
DEC = -05° 35' 28"
Distance = 1,300 ly

Visibility

Constellation = Orion
Months = November - March
Moonlight = Any

Guest Viewing

Eyepiece = 24mm
Guest Ability = Easy - Medium
Appearence = Blue-grey or green-grey, fuzzy, bright cloud in a crescent shape with a patch in the middle. 4-5 bright stars in the center cloud.
