M51 - "Whirlpool Galaxy" - NGC 5272

Location

R.A. = 13h 29m 52.7s
DEC = +47° 11' 43"
Distance = 24 million ly

Visibility

Constellation = Canes Venatici
Months = March - August
Moonlight = Less than 1/2 Moon

Guest Viewing

Eyepiece = 24mm
Guest Ability = Hard
Appearence = Faint, fuzy cores of both galaxies. Very faint disk around large galaxy. Spirals can be faintly visible with good eyes.

Frequently Asked Questions
