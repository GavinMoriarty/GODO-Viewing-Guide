M31 - "Andromeda Galaxy" - NGC 224

Location

R.A. = 00h 42m 44.3s
DEC = +41° 16' 09"
Distance = 2.537 million ly

Visibility

Constellation = Andromeda
Months = August - December
Moonlight = Less than 1/2 Moon

Guest Viewing

Eyepiece = 30mm 3"
Guest Ability = Medium-Hard
