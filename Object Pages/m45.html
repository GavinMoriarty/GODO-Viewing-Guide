Atlas (atlante) and Pleione are the parents of the Pleiades, Hyades, and Hyas. Atlas was a Titan ordered to hold up the heavans by Zeus. Pleione was a nymph who lived on Mount Kyllini in Arcadia, who was the protectress of sailing. The Pleiades were relentlessly pursued by Orion, the great hunter, and were turned into doves and then stars in the sky to escape Orions pursuits.
